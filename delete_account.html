Delete Account

If you wish to delete your account and all associated data from our fitness app, please follow one of the options below:

Option 1: In-App Deletion (if available)

You can delete your account directly from the app by going to: Settings → Account → Delete Account.

Option 2: Request by Email

If the above option is not available, you can send us an account deletion request at:

Email: support@[yourappname].com

Please include your registered email or Facebook ID used to sign in to help us verify your account.

Data Removal Policy

Once your request is received, your account and all related fitness data will be permanently deleted from our systems within 7 business days. This action cannot be undone.

Thank you for being part of our fitness community.
